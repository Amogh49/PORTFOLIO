About Me

I'm a dedicated computer science student with a unique passion for both photography and artificial intelligence. My journey began with capturing moments through the lens, which taught me to see patterns and beauty in the world around me.

This visual perspective naturally led me to explore AI and machine learning, where I'm fascinated by how computers can learn to recognize patterns, process images, and even create art. I'm currently working on projects that bridge these two worlds.

When I'm not coding algorithms or training models, you'll find me experimenting with new photography techniques or exploring how AI can enhance creative processes.
